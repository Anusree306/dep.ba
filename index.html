About the Department

The BA English Department offers a rich curriculum in literature, linguistics, and communication. With a legacy of academic excellence, we promote analytical skills, creative thinking, and a strong foundation in English studies.

Facilities

  • Modern language lab with audio-visual aids
  • Dedicated department library with over 2,000 titles
  • Wi-Fi enabled reading spaces
  • Seminar hall for lectures and presentations
  • Access to online journals and e-resources

Career & Academic Opportunities

Our graduates pursue careers in:

  • Teaching and Academia
  • Journalism and Media
  • Content Writing and Editing
  • Civil Services and Competitive Exams
  • Publishing and Public Relations
